The Indo-Pak Conflict: Understanding Terrorism & Retaliation

1. Origins of Conflict

Since 1947, India and Pakistan have engaged in multiple wars, primarily over Kashmir. Political tensions escalated into military conflicts and a long-lasting proxy war.

2. Surgical Strikes: A Strategic Military Response

On September 29, 2016, the Indian Army carried out surgical strikes across the Line of Control (LoC) in Pakistan-administered Kashmir. This was a response to the Uri attack, where 18 Indian soldiers were killed. The operation aimed to target militant camps and is considered one of India's most significant military actions in recent history.

3. Notable Terror Attacks

India has suffered multiple terrorist attacks that have left lasting scars on the nation. These attacks were often cross-border and targeted civilians and military personnel.

4. India's Military Response

India responded to these attacks with military operations aimed at dismantling terror networks:

  • Surgical Strikes (2016)
  • Balakot Air Strike (2019)
  • Strengthened border security

5. Impact on Civilians

Civilians, especially in Kashmir and border areas, have borne the brunt of these conflicts. Thousands of innocent lives have been lost due to terror attacks.
