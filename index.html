The Indo-Pak Conflict: Understanding Terrorism & Retaliation

Conflict Timeline

1. Origins of Conflict

Since 1947, India and Pakistan have engaged in multiple wars, primarily over Kashmir. Political tensions escalated into military conflicts and a long-lasting proxy war.

2. Surgical Strikes: A Strategic Military Response

On September 29, 2016, the Indian Army carried out surgical strikes in Pakistan-administered Kashmir as a response to the Uri attack. The operation aimed to target militant camps.

Uri Attack (2016)

18 Indian soldiers martyred in terrorist attack on army base

6. Hope for Peace

Despite decades of conflict, diplomatic efforts continue to seek peaceful resolution:

  • Simla Agreement (1972): Established bilateral framework for dispute resolution
  • Lahore Declaration (1999): Vowed to resolve Kashmir issue peacefully
  • Ceasefire Agreement (2021): Renewed commitment to LoC ceasefire
